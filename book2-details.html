1984

Author: George Orwell

A dystopian novel about totalitarianism and surveillance.

Published: 1949

Genre: Dystopian

ISBN: 978-0451524935

Summary: The story is set in a totalitarian society ruled by the Party and its leader, Big Brother. It follows Winston Smith, who works at the Ministry of Truth, as he begins to question the oppressive regime and seeks truth and freedom.
